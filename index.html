Application Process

How the Visa Process Works

A straightforward five-step process from application to approval.

1
Complete Application

Fill out the DS-160 online nonimmigrant visa application form with your personal information.

2
Upload Documents

Submit your passport, photo, financial statements, and supporting documents securely.

3
Pay Visa Fee

Pay the non-refundable MRV fee online. Fees vary by visa category and nationality.

4
Biometric Enrollment

Schedule and attend your biometric appointment at the nearest U.S. Embassy or Consulate.

5
Visa Decision

Receive your visa decision via email and track your passport delivery through this portal.

Visa Categories

Select Your Visa Type

Choose the category that best matches your purpose of travel to the United States.

B-1/B-2

Visitor Visa

For tourism, business meetings, medical treatment, or visiting friends and family.

MRV Fee: $185 USD
 F-1

Student Visa

For academic studies at accredited U.S. universities, colleges, and language programs.

MRV Fee: $185 USD
 H-1B

Work Visa

For specialty occupation workers sponsored by a U.S. employer in a qualifying field.

MRV Fee: $190 USD
 J-1

Exchange Visitor

For exchange programs, research scholars, professors, au pairs, and camp counselors.

MRV Fee: $160 USD
 O-1

Extraordinary Ability

For individuals with extraordinary ability or achievement in sciences, arts, education, business or athletics.

MRV Fee: $460 USD
 L-1

Intracompany Transfer

For managers, executives, or specialized knowledge workers transferred within the same company.

MRV Fee: $460 USD
